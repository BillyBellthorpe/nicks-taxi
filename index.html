About Nick

A Familiar Face You
Can Always Count On

Born and bred in Cornwall, Nick has been driving people around St Ives and the wider Penwith peninsula for over 15 years. He knows every backroad, shortcut, and parking spot in the area — so you'll always get there on time, the relaxed Cornish way.

Friendly, reliable, and always punctual — Nick's taxi is the first call for locals and returning visitors alike. Whether you need a 5-minute ride across town or a comfortable trip to the airport, you're in safe hands.

  • Cornwall Council licensed private hire driver with full public liability insurance
  • Clean, modern, air-conditioned vehicle — comfortable for all journeys
  • Cash and card payments accepted — no hidden charges, fixed prices on request
  • Pre-bookings welcome — popular for early morning airport runs
